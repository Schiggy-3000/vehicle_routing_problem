App development
- Google Play Console
    - Where apps are created (.aab file, testing, developer profile, goals of the app, ...)
- Google Play Store
    - Where apps can be downloaded
- Expo
    - Emulator to look at code changes (phone <-> "Webserver", instead of installing a new app version after every change)
    - Building app installation packages (.apk file: Android Package Kit)
    - Push .apk/.aab (Android App Bundle) to Google Play Console
    - .apk/.aab automatisch von Expo zu Google Play Console pushen
        - GCP > Service Account erstellen
        - Service Account bei Expo hinzufügen > Android & iOS credentials > Google Service Account Keys > JSON hochladen
        - Service Account als User bei Google Play Console hinzufügen: Users and permissions > Click on three dots > Invite new users
